Wow, a week-long hackathon that I attended, `Local Hack Day: Build` organized by `MLH (Major League Hacking)` between January-10 2021 January - 18 2021.

## Mode of Conduct

There we were given some common problems daily for 6- regular days and some challenges were given in the 3-day time frame. Besides, there were also weekly challenges that the participant had to solve according to the area of their interested domain.
One had to submit their hacks via the specified ways and that includes:
>
>- Submit hacks  on Devpost
>- Submit via Forms
>- Submit via Typeform

## Add-ons

Not only that, we were given the opportunity to connect with people around the world and to form a guild. The program was held in the EST time zone. Each day is followed by some workshops for problems given to us with the funniest activities in an exciting way. I had also played Chrome-Dino there with a lot of enjoyment.
